Data Submission Portal

Thank you for submitting data to Global Forest Watch, please follow the instructions below.

Instructions

Concession areas or plantations:

Submission must show the boundaries, not coordinates. Please specify in the attribute data or notes what the polygons refer to (e.g. planted areas, permitted areas, or concession areas)

RSPO ACOP submissions:

Submissions must show the boundaries for all global operations, certified and uncertified. Please indicate the planted areas and the permitted areas.

Processing facilities:

Data should be in the form of coordinates (e.g. lat/long). Please note the type of facility.

Please attach your data here, and please attach a table of attribute data to the right.
